AndCollective LLC Privacy Policy and Terms of Use

This policy was last updated on January 22, 2024.

AndCollective LLC (“we”, “MAP”, “us” or “our”) has a firm commitment to respect and protect the privacy of users of the MAP website (a “Site”). This AndCollective LLC Privacy Policy and Terms of Use (this “Privacy Policy”) describes how we collect, use, share, and protect the personal information of users of our Site. By accessing, using, registering with, or providing personal information to the Site, you explicitly accept, without limitation or qualification, the collection, use, storage and transfer (including international transfer) in the manner described in this Privacy Policy (as amended from time to time). THIS PRIVACY POLICY AFFECTS YOUR RIGHTS AND LIABILITIES UNDER THE LAW. If you do not agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy, do not access or use the Site or provide us with personal information. Doing so will result in your audition being automatically rejected.

The Site provides a range of content hosting and communication services, and seeks to connect talent, models, dancers, voice talent, hosts, potential contestants and show participants, and other artists (collectively, “Talent”) with professionals rendering professional services in motion picture, theatrical, radio, television and other entertainment enterprises (collectively, “Industry Professional Entities” and “Industry Professionals”).

Special notice to Talent

There are practical considerations relating to the need for Industry Professional Entities to review certain personal information, and to contact Talent for casting purposes. By registering and creating a profile on the Site you understand that you are affirmatively choosing to post personal information about yourself, including contact information such as your phone number and e-mail address, as well as physically identifying information such as your height, weight, hair, and eye color. You also have the option to post photographs, videos and other materials that may allow third parties to personally identify you. This information may be viewable outside of the Site via web search engines, which are outside of our control. By registering on the Site and submitting any such information, you acknowledge and agree that such personal information may be revealed to other Site users and the public, and that you are aware of the risks inherent in so doing.

Special note about minors

We are particularly concerned about the safety and privacy of all of our users, particularly individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under the age of 18, nor do we allow users under the age of 18 to directly establish their own profiles on the Site. All users under the age of 18 may only post profiles on the Site if they are represented by an adult agent or other authorized Industry Professional Entity, or by a parent or legal guardian who has registered on their behalf and who maintains the profile on their behalf (the “Registering Adult”). If we become aware that a profile has been established for an individual under the age of 18 without a Registering Adult, we will take steps to terminate such profile and remove any corresponding information.

Users under the age of 18 may monitor and modify their profile and personal information posted on the Site under the supervision of the Registering Adult.

This Privacy Policy applies solely to the Site. This Privacy Policy does not apply to any other website or any offline contact or communications external to the Site between MAP and any other company, you and MAP, or you and other registered users of the Site, or to information otherwise collected by third parties through social networking platforms.

Types of information we collect

We collect the following types of information on our Site:

  • Personal Information
  • Demographic Information
  • Log Data

Each of these terms are defined below. In each case, it is indicated on the Site whether any such information or data must be provided for you to access and use the requested service.

Personal information

“Personal information” is personal data that may be used to identify you, or any other person to whom the information may relate, personally. This may include name, title, occupation, postal or physical address, telephone number, e-mail address, and other information required to carry out a transaction, provide a service or deliver a product you have requested. Depending on where you are based, personal information may also include your Internet Protocol (IP) address and other information related to your computer. This may also include information about third parties, such as references or contacts (for example, a director or photographer with whom you have worked previously) that you provide to us if you have their express written consent to do so.

The collection and use of your personal information may be necessary for us to provide you with positive and customized experience on the Site, to provide you with a product or service, to communicate with you, or maintain our relationship with you. We do not collect your personal information unless you or someone who is expressly authorized by you (or if you are under 18, the Registering Adult) chooses to provide it to us.

You are not required to give us any personal information in order to read the content portions of our Site. However, please note that if you choose not to provide us with personal information, you may not be able to use certain functions and features of our Site.

We ask that you do not enter personal information into your profile, except in the fields provided for that information.

We will not collect sensitive information about you (such as information about your health or medical history; ethnic origin; political or religious beliefs; trade union, guild or other professional association memberships; sexual orientation; or criminal record) except where you have voluntarily provided it to us in the optional fields designated for that information or where its collection is required by law. Note that it is against the Privacy Policy to enter or otherwise provide any government identifier (such as a Social Security number, Tax ID number, or Medicare number) in your profile or any other area of the Site.

If we become aware that you have provided any government identifiers in your profile or anywhere else on the Site, we reserve the right to immediately delete that information.

Demographic Information

“Demographic Information” is information that you may submit or we may collect that is neither personal nor financial. This is information that is not unique to you in the sense that it refers to selected population characteristics, and may include but is not limited to your zip code, postal code, hometown, gender, user name, race or ethnicity, membership in professional guilds such as the Screen Actors Guild, your age, height and weight.

Online correspondence and communications

Subject to the terms of this Privacy Policy, we may collect and store your online correspondence that you send to us and the content of online communications exchanged between you and other Site users using the tools available on the Site.

How we collect your information

You provide most of the information that we collect. We also collect your information from the social media accounts that you provide us with a link to.

We collect data and process data when you take the following actions:

  • Register a new user account or revise the information on your existing user account;
  • Register to be a participant in one of our partners’ programming;
  • When we interview you by phone calls, online video calls, or in person, including recording videos as part of your audition; or
  • When you directly contact us regarding questions or support concerns.

Industry Professionals or other third parties with access to our searchable talent database may be located in countries whose data protection legislation may not be equivalent to the laws in your country. For example, your profile may be viewed and accessed by Industry Professionals outside the European Economic Area who have access to our searchable talent database.
